About Us

Timber Run Builders is your one-stop remodeling and property maintenance service company in Saint Louis. We provide professional, high quality construction service in the Saint Louis County area. Timber Run Builders takes pride in offering a vast variety of remodeling services along with decades of construction knowledge that we provide to every project at an extremely competitive low price to all of our customers. All of our accomplishments are embodied in many successful projects completed to date and with satisfied customer testimonials.
All of our customers enjoy peace of mind knowing that we guarantee all of our work to be completed on time and that Timber Run Builders is fully insured.
